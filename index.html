Rubik's Cube Move Notations

L: the left face.

R: the right face

B: the bottom face

T: the top face

F: the front face

K: the back face

M, E, S are the faces in between

Lower case indicates the reversed rotation.

Note:You can use your mouse to rotate the cube as a whole to

any desired angle, and you are able to control the rotations of

each plane using the buttons provided here. The faces are in fact

relative to your view of the cube.
